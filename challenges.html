Challenges in Cybersecurity

The African Cybersecurity Landscape

In Africa, the journey toward robust cybersecurity is filled with challenges, including:

  • Limited access to cutting-edge tools and resources.
  • Insufficient cybersecurity training and awareness.
  • High reliance on open-source tools, which may require extensive customization.

Despite these obstacles, we continue to innovate, learn, and build secure systems that rival global standards.
